Tone

A well-chosen tone helps establish a brand's personality and voice, creating a consistent user experience that resonates with the target audience.

Build trust and credibility

As government organizations we must speak to the citizens of California. We have a very diverse population and we need to take that into account in our writing. Below are some of the values that are at the core of our design system.

Consistency

A consistent tone of voice across all website content helps build trust and credibility. Users are more likely to trust a brand if they can anticipate the language and style they'll encounter.

Authenticity

A well-defined tone helps the brand communicate its values and principles, attracting users who align with those values.

Clarity

Choosing the right tone ensures that the message is clear and easy to understand, preventing confusion and building confidence.
