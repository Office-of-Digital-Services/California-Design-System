Footer

A website footer is the section at the very end of a webpage. It often contains helpful links and important information, such as:

• Contact information so people can get in touch with you. • Navigation links for links to key pages, like "About Us," "Privacy Policy," or "Accessibility." • Social media buttons that take you to the website's social media profiles. • Copyright showing who owns the website content and the year it was last updated. • Am I missing anything?
